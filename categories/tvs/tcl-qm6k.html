TCL QM6K TV Review

February 24, 2025
4.5/5 - Editor's Choice

Quick Verdict

The TCL QM6K delivers exceptional picture quality with its Mini LED technology, making it a strong competitor in the premium TV market at a more accessible price point.

Pros

  • Excellent Mini LED backlighting
  • Vibrant HDR performance
  • Google TV integration
  • Great gaming features
  • Competitive pricing

Cons

  • Slight blooming in dark scenes
  • Audio could be better
  • Limited viewing angles
