JBL Bar 300 Soundbar Review

February 23, 2025
4/5

Quick Verdict

The JBL Bar 300 delivers impressive sound in a compact form factor, making it perfect for small to medium-sized rooms. With Dolby Atmos and MultiBeam technology, it creates an immersive audio experience.

Pros

  • Compact design
  • Dolby Atmos support
  • MultiBeam technology
  • Easy setup
  • Built-in Alexa

Cons

  • No separate subwoofer
  • Limited bass response
  • Premium price point
